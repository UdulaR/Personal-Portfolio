How I Work

I am currently learning about user interface and user experience design through SEG 3125 at the University of Ottawa. I also use resources from the Nielsen Norman Group to learn about professional usability standards and industry best practices.

User-Centred Design

I begin by understanding the intended users, their needs, and the problems the interface needs to solve.

Prototyping

I develop wireframes and prototypes before building the final product so ideas can be tested and improved early.

Accessibility

I aim to create responsive and accessible interfaces that are clear, readable, and easy for a wide range of users to navigate.
